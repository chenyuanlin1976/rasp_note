# Lunar phase

A lunar phase or Moon phase is the apparent shape of the Moons day and night phases of the lunar day as viewed from afar.  
[lunar phase](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lunar_phase)

## Moon Phases

1. New Moon
2. Waxing crescent
3. First quarter
4. Waxing gibbous
5. Full Moon
6. Waning gibbous
7. Last quarter
8. Waning crescent
